5k To A Better Day

My first 5k run showed me that there's so much to do, and that there are so many people who are also enjoying the simple things in life. Many of the people I knew had no interest in the idea of paying money to run. Being a part of a community was the best part about 5k runs. Not just the awesome run, but the amazing interactions. I'm no marathon prodigy, however having fun while burning some calories was definitely up my alley.
My favorite 5k was the Mud Factor because it not only required us to run, but also required some dexterity and strength. Running hills, climbing walls, and crawling in mud was something to remember. Lots of laughs were something to remember.
