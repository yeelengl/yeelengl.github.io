Bon Chon

If I could recommend a place to grab a bite, it would always be Bon Chon located at the Campus Point. The Korean cuisine brings a unique taste pallet with a variety of choices to choose from. Bon Chon is a great social space to enjoy food and have a good laugh. They are known for their crispy Korean fried chicken (wings or legs). Highly recommend.

Grill Masters

I can always find myself coming back to this smoke bbq join. I wouldn't necessarily say their smoke bbq is the best, however if I could recommend an item off the menu, that would have to be the Mini Matty burger. It is not your typical cheese burger or gourmet burger. The burger has a sense of dominance loaded with protein consisting of a double patty bacon stripes with smoked pork shoulder, and best of all... smoked bbq brisket. Oh, I guess I should include that it does have lettuce a slice of tomato and maybe like 2 pickles. Overall it is a must try burger, and when paired with their house made bbq sauce, "that would be perfection"
