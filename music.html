Music For The Soul

Throughout my entire life, music has been apart of me everywhere I've gone. I'm no professional, but I've been given the opportunity to showcase myself in front of numerous crowds. I never once thought I'd be able to play on a grand stage, however I enjoyed being able to share my love for music when given the opportunity.
Since high school til now, I've been able to continue enjoying music over the years. Although it is not a priority, my connection with music follows me everywhere I go. It has been a good ice breaker or shock to many people. As stated before, I am no great singer, but I do love a little karaoke!
