Hiking At A Pace

Staying busy was a new habit I picked up. Although the weather was great for biking, it was also amazing for hiking. Sight seeing is commonly known for most people when it comes to hiking, but for me, it's the mental and physical challenge that gets me fired up. The scenery is just a bonus for me.
There are many places I'd like to tackle and see, however it can be very time consuming having to drive and in this day and age... gas prices. Hiking had helped me take on something different to break up the repetitive activities I did before. It was a nice way to help slow things down a bit and enjoy things at a slower pace. Being stuck on a hill on two feet is much easier than being stuck on a hill on a bike (especially when you're clipped in).
