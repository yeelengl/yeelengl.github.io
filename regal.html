Regal: Is This My Movie?

While being a student, I took a part-time job at Regal Cinema (the movie theater). You'd think it'd be quite easy going working at a family entertainment facility, however boy was it a mess. As a floor staff I got to experience all 4 positions starting as an usher. After excelling as an usher, I was moved up to a concessionist and the interactions with customers was the stress that made me motivated to get out. However, over time, I was able to move into the box office offering ticket transactions. Things became hectic again when being asked to run the guest services. After finishing my 2 year degree, I decided to leave and take a position that would require less customer interaction all while looking for a new position. I landed a simple warehouse job at the GAP Inc warehouse.
