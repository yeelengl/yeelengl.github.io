Planet Fitness For Noobies

January 19, 2019 I decided to join my very first gym! As any other person new to the gym, the gym-timidation was real. I was intimidated of the other gym goers, and I was insecured of being in my own body in a setting like that. Luckily I had my brother who helped push me everyday to keep the consistency.
Day by day I was in the gym sweating from 8:30pm to 11pm. I caught on really fast with how to do the exercises. I was locked in. I was hungry for more. My free time was always about learning more knowledge on the science behind working out. My obession for the gym sparked when I saw my first 6 pounds of probably water drop. It was a great start, and my research from types of exercises to nutrition continued to grow.
